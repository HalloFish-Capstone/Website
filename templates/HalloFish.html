Welcome to HalloFish

Our website, HalloFish, is an innovative solution to assist fish farmers in quickly and accurately detecting fish diseases. We understand that fish diseases are one of the main obstacles in freshwater fish farming, leading to significant economic losses. By utilizing the latest technology in image recognition, we have developed a website application that enables fish farmers to detect fish diseases simply by uploading photos.

Why Choose Us

There are several reasons why HalloFish is a good choice for detecting fish diseases and providing related information

Speed and Accuracy

HalloFish utilizes advanced image recognition technology to detect fish diseases quickly and accurately. By uploading photos of infected fish, you will receive detection results promptly, enabling swift actions to prevent disease spread and reduce economic losses.

Comprehensive Information

HalloFish not only provides disease detection results but also grants access to a comprehensive database of information about various fish diseases, including prevention measures, recommended treatments, and solutions to avoid disease spread. This helps fish farmers better understand the diseases and take effective steps to address them in their fish farming operations.

Accessibility

Being a web application, HalloFish can be accessed via mobile devices or computers with an internet connection. This makes it convenient for fish farmers to use the application wherever and whenever they need it, without requiring additional software installations.
